Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before listing.

Is it free to list my vehicle?expand_more

Yes, listing on DriveGO is completely free. We only charge a 20% commission when your vehicle is successfully rented. No upfront or monthly fees.

What documents do I need?expand_more

You need your vehicle's RC Book, valid insurance certificate, and bank account details for payouts. All reviewed securely by our team.

How does the auto-lock system protect my vehicle?expand_more

Our time-based system only reveals vehicle access details to the renter during their confirmed booking window. Before and after — all details are locked. No hardware required.

Can I set my own pricing and availability?expand_more

Yes. You control your daily rate, minimum rental duration, available dates and blackout periods. Update anytime from your host dashboard.

Can I list multiple vehicles?expand_more

Yes! Many hosts list entire fleets. With 6+ vehicles you qualify for our Fleet Partner program with additional benefits and a dedicated account manager.
