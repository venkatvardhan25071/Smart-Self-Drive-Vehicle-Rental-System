help Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my booking? expand_more

You can cancel your booking directly from the Activity page by clicking "Details" on an active booking and scrolling to the bottom to find the Cancel button.

When will I get my refund? expand_more

Refunds for rejected bookings or cancellations are processed instantly from our end and typically reflect in your account within 3-5 business days depending on your bank.

Can I change the vehicle color? expand_more

Yes, you can change the color during the booking process. Once booked, you must contact support to request a color change, subject to availability.
