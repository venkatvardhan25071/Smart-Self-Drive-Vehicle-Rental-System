quiz Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a vehicle?

chevron_right
Navigate to the home screen, select a vehicle, choose your pickup/return dates and location, then confirm and pay. It takes under 2 minutes!

What documents are required?

chevron_right
You'll need to upload a valid Driving License and Aadhaar Card during your first booking for verification.

How does billing work?

chevron_right
You're charged per day based on the vehicle rate. Taxes and fees are shown at checkout. A refundable security deposit may apply.

Can I cancel my booking?

chevron_right
Yes, you can cancel from the Activity page. Cancellations made 24 hours before pickup are eligible for a full refund.

What if the vehicle breaks down?

chevron_right
Call us immediately at our 24/7 helpline. Roadside assistance is included for all DriveGO bookings at no extra cost.
